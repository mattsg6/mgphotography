About Matthew Gilmore

Who is Matt?

Matthew Gilmore is a film photographer and college student in the triangle area. Having been around his father's photography business for many years, Matthew began his own photographic endeavours and purchased a film camera. This camera, a Pentax k-1000, is the camera he continues to use because of its versatility and ease of use. In addition to his photo-taking adventures, Matthew studies computer science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

What does Matt do?

One of Matthew's favorite places to be is in nature. Whether it's at the nearby Eno River or across the North Atlantic in Iceland, Matthew loves taking pictures outside. Whenever he can't go exploring he enjoys taking candids of his lovely family and friends. Please note, Matthew is not a hireable photographer. He just wants to share his hobby and joy with the world.
