Contact

Purchase

If you wish to purchase a print from any of the collections please fill out the contact form below as follows: enter your email; the collection it's from in the subject line; a description of the photo in the inquiry box. I try to be timely in my responses but please be patient. Your patronage is appreciated.

Question or Concerns

If you have a question or concern - regarding the website, my photography, or something else - please fill out the contact form as follows: enter your email; write "Question/Concern" in the subject line; your question and/or concern in the inquiry box. Please be patient as I respond to these messages.

Other

If you have a message you'd like to send me that doesn't fit into the other two categories then fill out the contact form as follows: enter your email; subject line takes the form "Other - Subject" where 'Subject' is a short description of the message; your message in the inquiry box. If the message warrants a response I will try to be timely.
